Basic ROTEM - Step-by-Step

  1. FIBTEM A5 - is it < 10mm?
  2. EXTEM A5 - is it < 35mm?
  3. EXTEM CT - is it > 80s?
  4. Shape/ML - is it tailing off or ML > 5%?

Basic ROTEM - Algorithm

1. PPH, Trauma, or ML > 5%

  • Indicates HYPERFIBRINOLYSIS
  • Give TXA 1g

2. FIBTEM A5 < 10mm

  • Indicates LOW FIBRINOGEN
  • Give Cryoprecipitate 10U

3. EXTEM A5 < 35mm

  • Indicates LOW PLATELETS
  • Give Platelets 1 pool

4. EXTEM CT > 80s

  • If EXTEM CT > 80s + FIBTEM A5 > 10mm
    • Indicates LOW CLOTTING FACTORS with NORMAL FIBRINOGEN
    • Give FFP 2U
  • If EXTEM CT 80-140s + FIBTEM A5 < 10mm
    • Indicates LOW FIBRINOGEN ONLY (without low clotting factors necessarily)
    • Give Cryoprecipitate 10U and reassess
  • If EXTEM CT > 140s + FIBTEM A5 < 10mm
    • Indicates LOW CLOTTING FACTORS and LOW FIBRINOGEN
    • Give Cryoprecipitate 10U + FFP 2U

4. EXTEM CT > 80s

  • Is FIBTEM A5 > 10mm?
    • Indicates LOW CLOTTING FACTORS with NORMAL FIBRINOGEN
    • Give FFP 2U
  • Is FIBTEM A5 < 10mm?
    • If EXTEM CT 80-140s:
      • Indicates LOW FIBRINOGEN ONLY (without low clotting factors necessarily)
      • Give Cryoprecipitate 10U and reassess
    • If EXTEM CT > 140s:
      • Indicates LOW CLOTTING FACTORS and LOW FIBRINOGEN
      • Give Cryoprecipitate 10U + FFP 2U
