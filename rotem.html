Basic ROTEM - Algorithm

PPH or Trauma, or ML > 5% Hyperfibrinolysis TXA 1g
FIBTEM A5 < 10mm Low fibrinogen Cryoprecipitate 10U
EXTEM A5 < 35mm Insufficient platelets Platelets 1 pool
EXTEM CT > 80s + FIBTEM A5 > 10mm Low clotting factors with NORMAL fibrinogen FFP 2U
EXTEM CT 80-140s + FIBTEM A5 < 10mm Likely low fibrinogen only Cryoprecipitate 10U and reassess
EXTEM CT > 140s + FIBTEM A5 < 10mm Low clotting factors AND Low fibrinogen Cryoprecipitate 10U + FFP 2U
