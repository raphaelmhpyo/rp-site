Basic ROTEM - Algorithm

PPH, Trauma, or ML %gt 5%

  • Indicates Hyperfibrinolysis
  • TXA 1g

FIBTEM A5 < 10mm

  • Indicates Low Fibrinogen
  • Cryoprecipitate 10U

EXTEM A5 < 35mm

  • Indicates Low Platelets
  • Platelets 1 pool

EXTEM CT > 80s

  • If EXTEM CT %gt 80s + FIBTEM A5 %gt 10mm
    • Indicates Low Clotting factors with Normal Fibrinogen
    • FFP 2U
  • If EXTEM CT 80-140s + FIBTEM A5 %lt 10mm
    • Indicates Low Fibrinogen only (without low clotting factors necessarily)
    • Cryoprecipitate 10U and reassess
  • If EXTEM CT %gt 140s + FIBTEM A5 %lt 10mm
    • Indicates Low Clotting factors and Low Fibrinogen
    • Cryoprecipitate 10U + FFP 2U
PPH or Trauma, or ML > 5% Hyperfibrinolysis TXA 1g
FIBTEM A5 < 10mm Low fibrinogen Cryoprecipitate 10U
EXTEM A5 < 35mm Insufficient platelets Platelets 1 pool
EXTEM CT > 80s + FIBTEM A5 > 10mm Low clotting factors with NORMAL fibrinogen FFP 2U
EXTEM CT 80-140s + FIBTEM A5 < 10mm Likely low fibrinogen only Cryoprecipitate 10U and reassess
EXTEM CT > 140s + FIBTEM A5 < 10mm Low clotting factors AND Low fibrinogen Cryoprecipitate 10U + FFP 2U
